About Us


Full Circle Support is family owned and operated. The business was established in 2005 by two mothers of special needs children.


Jim and Lisa Batty are the owners of the company. They are the proud parents of three boys. Lisa began working with the disabled community providing respite for parents like herself when her youngest son was diagnosed with autism and several other medical issues over 20 years ago. She is the CEO of the company which she co-founded in 2005. Jim has retired from his primary profession and working together they are dedicated to fulfilling Full Circle Support's mission of giving back to the disabled community

Debbie McDermott has been involved in helping people with disabilities for over 20 years. Debbie co-founded Full Circle Support in 2005. She is a mother of four, wife, and was CFO of the company. She has since retired and is serving as a consultant for the business.
